Privacy Policy

Applicable App: OneMore | Last Updated: July 8, 2026

Thank you for using "OneMore" (the "App"). The App is developed and operated by Yu Te Lu ("we", "us"). We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information related to you.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not require an account, and we do not actively collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

  • Game Data: Your game scores, progress, and settings are stored locally on your device only and are never uploaded to our servers.
  • Advertising Data: The App contains ads served by third-party advertising services (for example, rewarded ads in the "watch an ad to revive" feature). These third-party services may collect device identifiers (such as the IDFA), IP address (which may be used to infer approximate location), device model, ad interaction data, and diagnostic data (such as crash and performance data) to provide and improve their advertising services.

2. Third-Party Advertising Services

The App uses third-party advertising services (such as Google AdMob) to display ads. The collection and use of data by these third parties are governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob: https://policies.google.com/privacy

On iOS devices, under Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework, the system will ask for your permission before any advertising service tracks your activity across apps. You can change this permission at any time in Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking. If you decline tracking, you can still use all features of the App; the ads you see may simply be less relevant to your interests.

For users in the European Economic Area and the UK, the App displays a consent form (Google User Messaging Platform) before serving personalized ads. You can change or withdraw your consent at any time via "Privacy Options" in the App's settings.

3. How Information Is Used

Advertising-related data is used only to:

  • Display ads and provide ad-based reward features such as "watch an ad to revive";
  • Measure ad performance and prevent ad fraud.

We do not sell any data to third parties. Depending on your region, you may have rights to access or delete data collected by our advertising partners; please refer to Google's privacy policy or contact us.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your game scores and settings are stored locally on your device only. If you delete the App, this data will be deleted along with it and cannot be recovered.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is rated 13+ on the App Store and is not directed at children under 13. It is a casual puzzle game that does not involve any real-money transactions or gambling. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child may have provided us with personal information, please contact us using the details below.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
